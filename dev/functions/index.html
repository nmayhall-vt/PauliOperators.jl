Base.adjoint — Method
Base.adjoint(p::Pauli)


Pauli{N}(s,z,x)  =  s ⋅ z₁...|x₁... 
                 =  s ⋅ i^-θs ⋅ P₁⊗...⊗Pₙ
                 =  coeff ⋅ P₁⊗...⊗Pₙ

Since the PauliBasis is Hermitian, we have that Pauli' = coeff' ⋅ P₁⊗...⊗Pₙ
